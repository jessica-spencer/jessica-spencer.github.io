Why Does Summer Search Matter?

Our students come from families that make less than $30,000 a year, which is under the NYC poverty line.

We provide career-readiness workshops, mentorship, and financial wellness courses that help lead them on a path towards better educational, employment and financial futures.

Educational Attainment

Higher education gives you a better chance of escaping poverty. Those with a bachelor's degree earn, on average, 50% more than those with a high school diploma or less. Our alumni outpace the national average on educational attainment.

  • 99% alumni graduate from High School
  • Over 85% matriculate into college
  • 82.6% complete their Bachelor's Degree

Employment Opportunities

Summer Search New York City has a proven track record of working with partnering organizations for Summer Internships.

100% of our students are above the NYC poverty threshold for a family of 4, demonstrating improved economic outcomes.

Financial Well-Being

We begin supporting participants' financial well-being, one of our long-term outcomes, in high school.

86% of our students consistently pay their bills on time, demonstrating strong financial responsibility.
