Empowering Future Leaders Through Summer Search

Our students come from families that make less than $30,000 a year, below the NYC poverty line. We provide career-readiness workshops, mentorship, and financial wellness courses to create pathways for better educational, employment and financial futures.

Educational Impact

82.6% of participants complete their Bachelor's Degree

Program Impact Metrics

Career Success

68% of our students attain full-time employment within 6 months of completing their post-secondary pathway. Our program provides comprehensive support including:

  • Career-readiness workshops
  • Internship placements
  • Professional mentorship
  • Networking opportunities

Financial Well-Being

86% of our students consistently pay their bills on time, demonstrating strong financial responsibility. Our financial wellness program includes budgeting workshops, financial literacy training, and ongoing support.
